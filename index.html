Israel Attacks Ship

Israel is suspected to have been behind an explosion on the Iranian ship Shahr e Kord. The Iranian container ship was reportedly hit by an explosive object en route between Rajaei, Iran to Europe. While nobody on board was injured, the attack resulted in a small fire forcing the ship to dock in the Syrian port of Latakia. Iranian state media promised that legal actions would follow in an attempt to bring to justice what they considered to be a "terrorist act." Israel has not commented on any involvement in the attack.
